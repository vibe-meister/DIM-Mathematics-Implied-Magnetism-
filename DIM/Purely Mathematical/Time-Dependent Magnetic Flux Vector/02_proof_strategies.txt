Time-Dependent Magnetic Flux Vector — Proof Strategies

Lemma 1 (Orthogonal projection estimates)
- With orthonormal {1/T ∫ f_i f_j dt = δ_ij}, coefficients c_i=⟨r,f_i⟩ minimize ∥r−Σ c_i f_i∥².

Lemma 2 (Principal sinusoid recovery)
- Projections a_c=(2/T)∫ y cos(ωt)dt, a_s=(2/T)∫ y sin(ωt)dt recover B0,ϕ0 in noiseless case; small-noise bias O(σ²).

Lemma 3 (Leakage bounds)
- Leakage λ_i := |⟨cos(ωt+ϕ), f_i⟩|; if λ_i=0 for all i, principal and auxiliary subspaces decouple; otherwise bound bias by Σ λ_i|B_i|.

Lemma 4 (Variance)
- For white noise σ², Var(c_i)=σ²; Var(B̂0), Var(ϕ̂0) given by standard sinusoidal parameter estimation approximations.

Lemma 5 (Regularization)
- Ridge/LASSO shrink variance in collinear designs; trade bias vs variance; select via cross‑validation or information criteria.

Validation
- Simulate with known {f_i}, ω, ϕ0 to verify identifiability, leakage, and CI coverage.
