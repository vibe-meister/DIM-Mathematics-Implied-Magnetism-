Magnetic Scalar Waves — Proof Strategies

Lemma 1 (Energy Identity)
- Multiply Φ_tt − c²ΔΦ = 0 by Φ_t and integrate; use divergence theorem; track boundary flux; conclude dE/dt=boundary work.

Lemma 2 (Dispersion Relation)
- Substitute plane wave ansatz; obtain ω=c|k|; show finite propagation speed.

Lemma 3 (Existence/Uniqueness)
- Use semigroup theory or energy method for homogeneous BCs; uniqueness from energy identity; stability from Grönwall‑type arguments (trivial here with no forcing).

Lemma 4 (Green’s Function)
- d=3: G(x,t)=δ(t−|x|/c)/(4π|x|)H(t); derive solution via convolution with initial data or sources.

Extensions
- Damped wave: energy decay rate; derive dE/dt=−2γ∥Φ_t∥² plus boundary terms.
- Variable c(x): use microlocal/GO tools; rays obey Hamiltonian flow |p|=1/c(x).

Validation
- Check energy conservation numerically and wave speed via manufactured solutions.
